Protein and Hormones: Why Women Need Enough

Hormones regulate everything from mood to metabolism, and protein plays a vital role in their production. Amino acids act as building blocks for hormones like insulin and estrogen. Without sufficient protein, hormone levels can fluctuate, affecting energy, skin health and mental clarity.

For women, this means including protein at each meal can help stabilize blood sugar and mood. Aim for a mix of animal and plant sources to obtain a range of amino acids and micronutrients.

Balance is key. Overemphasis on protein can crowd out other nutrients【568182238129158†L334-L343】, so pair protein with colorful vegetables, whole grains and healthy fats.
