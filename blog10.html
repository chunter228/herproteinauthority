Sip & Enjoy: Why Women Love Koia’s Plant‑Based Shakes

Between work, family and social commitments, busy women need quick and nutritious options. Koia’s plant‑based shakes offer 18‑20 grams of protein per bottle, along with fiber and low sugar. They’re free of dairy, gluten and soy, making them suitable for many dietary needs.

Flavor matters too—Koia comes in delicious varieties like Cookies n’ Cream and Chocolate Peanut Butter. Enjoy them on their own, blend them into smoothies or pour them over granola for a protein‑boosted breakfast.

While convenient, remember that shakes complement—not replace—whole foods. Use Koia as a tool to meet your protein goals on hectic days or after workouts, and pair it with fruits, vegetables and healthy fats to round out your nutrition.
