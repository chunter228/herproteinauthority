High‑Protein Breakfast Ideas to Power Your Day

Breakfast sets the tone for the day. Starting with protein can keep you full and prevent mid‑morning energy crashes. Try a Greek yogurt parfait with berries and granola, a tofu scramble with veggies, or overnight oats made with chia seeds and almond butter.

Smoothie bowls are another popular option. Blend a frozen banana with spinach, a scoop of protein powder and unsweetened almond milk, then top with nuts and seeds. Or, for convenience, pour a Koia shake over cereal for a quick breakfast on the go.

Aim for 20‑25 grams of protein at breakfast to support muscle maintenance and stable blood sugar. Combine it with fiber and healthy fats for lasting satiety.
